Privacy Policy for WhyBot

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

Welcome to WhyBot, a Discord bot inspired by historical Turkish TV series and culture. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains what information WhyBot may collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

WhyBot may collect and temporarily process the following information when used in Discord servers:

Discord User IDs
Discord Server IDs
Channel IDs
Message content used in commands
Bot interaction data
Basic usage logs for debugging and moderation

WhyBot does not collect:

Passwords
Email addresses
Payment information
Private Discord account credentials
2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used only for:

Running bot commands
Improving bot performance
Preventing abuse and spam
Debugging errors
Server moderation features

Your data is never sold to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Some data may be temporarily stored to allow WhyBot features to function correctly. Data is stored securely and only kept as long as necessary.

4. Third-Party Services

WhyBot operates through:

Discord API
Hosting providers or databases used by the developer

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Discord Privacy Policy
GitHub Privacy Statement
5. Children's Privacy

WhyBot is not intended for children under 13 years old or below the minimum digital consent age in your country.

6. Your Rights

Server owners or users may request:

Data deletion
Information about stored data
Removal of server-specific data

Requests can be made through the official WhyBot support contact.

7. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of WhyBot means you agree to the latest version.

8. Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the WhyBot developer through GitHub or Discord.
